# Summary

I searched for storage box on Thingiverse. I find O3D great design the Modular Drawers 2.0
but its too big for my small things (M2,M3 screws nuts, bullet connector etc.) Because of this i made the smaller version. The drawers knob originnaly designed by fipsthedog (i just use it :D).
Unfortunately the mini drawers not compatible with the big brother.

The dimensions: 

- Single frame: 40x40x100mm
- Double frame: 80x40x100mm
- Single drawer: 33x33x97,5
- Double drawer: 73x33x97,5

You find 2 different type for the single drawer. 1 with double aperture and 1 with quad aperture. The double version had only one version with 2 aperture.
If you wanna make 1 aperture just cut out the middle wall on the 2 aperture drawer. This wall need to keep the side walls straight during the printing.
The Frame don't need any adhesive to hold it together due the interlocking system.
I printing it on my Anet A8 FDM printer.

Printing material white & clear ABS
I used a cheap ABS ~12€/kg and it works well
